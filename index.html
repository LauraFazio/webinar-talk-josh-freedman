Our clients

Everyone calls it a soft skill. It's the hardest one.

Reducing emotional intelligence to a soft skill is the fastest way to misunderstand it.

Across 160+ countries, Joshua Freedman has measured emotional intelligence, and the data reveals an "Emotional Recession": global EQ is falling, while trust, attention and the ability to listen become the rarest, most in-demand resource inside teams.

This is where your work comes in: no communication, sales conversation or presentation works without reading the person in front of you. Join Maurizio in this live interview on the MLC podcast, one hour to turn emotional intelligence from a word into a real skill, and walk away knowing how to use it the very next morning.

The guest

Joshua Freedman

CEO & co-founder of Six Seconds · Author of Emotion Rules

Joshua leads Six Seconds, the world's largest network dedicated to emotional intelligence, active in over 150 countries. A Master Certified Coach (ICF) and author of seven books, he created reference psychometric tools (the SEI assessment) and leads State of the Heart, the global study that has measured EQ since 2011.

He coined the concept of the "Emotional Recession": the steady, measurable decline of emotional intelligence worldwide, and what it means for leadership, trust and the ability to communicate.
